# Migrating Humanscale & Steelcase Products to UPLIFT Desk

### Catalog Migration • Product Data Management • E-commerce Operations

---

## Overview

UPLIFT Desk maintained a large catalog of third-party office furniture through its sister site, The Human Solution (THS). Many of these products existed only on the legacy THS storefront, while others were duplicated across both THS and UPLIFT Desk with inconsistent pricing, content, imagery, and product configurations.

This project focused on modernizing that catalog by migrating Humanscale and Steelcase products from THS to UPLIFT Desk's primary e-commerce platform. My work centered on validating product data, rebuilding listings, coordinating cross-functional implementation, and ensuring each product could be successfully purchased before launch.

## Business Challenge

The Human Solution operated on a legacy e-commerce platform that contained much of UPLIFT Desk's third-party dropship catalog. Over time, product information became fragmented across multiple systems.

Common issues included:

- Outdated product information and imagery
- Inconsistent pricing and configuration options
- Duplicate maintenance across multiple websites
- Vendor information that had changed since products were originally created
- Products that were difficult for customers to discover on UPLIFT's primary storefront

Without a structured migration process, inaccurate product data could negatively impact purchasing, customer confidence, and ongoing catalog maintenance.

---

## My Role

I was responsible for migrating third-party products from The Human Solution into UPLIFT Desk's modern product ecosystem.

Responsibilities included:

- Auditing existing product listings
- Validating pricing and product configurations
- Verifying specifications against vendor documentation
- Creating new BigCommerce product listings
- Building supporting content in Contentful
- Testing purchasing workflows
- Coordinating implementation changes with the Web Development team when platform updates were required
- Publishing completed listings after validation

---

## Approach / Implementation

Each migration followed a structured workflow designed to ensure product accuracy before launch.

### 1. Audit Existing Products

Reviewed legacy THS listings for:

- Product content
- Images
- Specifications
- Metadata
- Pricing
- Configuration options

### 2. Validate Against Vendor Resources

Compared existing information against:

- Vendor websites
- Product documentation
- Updated pricing
- Product catalogs
- Internal records

When discrepancies existed, I worked with internal stakeholders and vendor representatives to determine the correct product data.

### 3. Rebuild the Product

After validation, I rebuilt the product within UPLIFT's ecosystem by:

- Creating new BigCommerce listings
- Building modular content within Contentful
- Configuring product options
- Updating imagery
- Applying current purchasing rules

### 4. Functional Testing

Before launch I verified:

- Product configuration logic
- Purchasing workflows
- Cart behavior
- Hidden staging products
- End-to-end test orders

Any issues requiring platform changes were coordinated with the Web Development team before publication.

## Results & Impact

The migration successfully modernized a significant portion of UPLIFT Desk's third-party catalog while improving long-term maintainability.

Key outcomes included:

- Personally migrated **28 of the 32 currently live Humanscale and Steelcase product listings**
- Improved product accuracy through vendor validation and documentation review
- Reduced duplicate catalog maintenance by consolidating products onto UPLIFT Desk's primary storefront
- Increased discoverability of third-party products
- Improved purchasing reliability through comprehensive testing
- Established a repeatable migration process for future catalog modernization projects
